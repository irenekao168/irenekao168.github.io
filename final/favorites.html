FAVORITE RECIPES
STRAWBERRY SHORTCAKE
  1. Beat the egg yolks (3), sugar (1/4 cup), salt (1/8 tspn), honey (1 tspn),
    vanilla (1/4 tspn), and water (1/3 cup) together
  2. Beat the egg whites (3) until peaks form
  3. Add half of the mixture from step 2 to the mixture from step 1 and fold
  4. Sift cake flour (1/2 cup) and baking powder (1/4 tspn) into the mixture
    from previous step, add vegetable oil
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F for 15-25 minutes (6-8 inch pans only)
  6. Slice the cake into layers, add cold heavy cream (whip 2 cups until
    peaks form) and sliced strawberries between the layers
  7. Assemble the layers and frost the sides/top of the cake, decorate with
    any amount of strawberries
HOT FUDGE SUNDAE
  1. Add cream (1 cup), sugar (1/2 cup), salt (1/4 tspn), butter (1/2 cup),
    then simmer
  2. Add chocolate (6 ounces) into mixture from previous step and stir until
    melted, then add the cocoa powder (1/4 cup) to make the fudge
  3. Choose ice cream flavors of your choice and add 1 or more scoops of
    the ice cream to a bowl/glass
  4. Drizzle the fudge onto the ice cream
  5. Add some whipped cream on top of the sundae and add sprinkles,
    nuts, fruits, or other toppings
HOT FUDGE SUNDAE
  1. Beat the cake flour (3 cup), salt (1 tspn), baking powder (2 tspn), and
    baking soda (2 tspn) together
  2. Beat butter (1 cup) and sugar (2 cups) until it becomes smooth (3 min),
    add in 3 eggs, 2 egg whites, and vanilla extract, then beat mixture for 2
    min
  3. Pour mixture from previous step into cake pans and bake on 350
    degrees F for 23-26 minutes
  4. Beat butter (1/2 cup) on medium speed until it becomes creamy (2
    min) to make buttercream frosting
  5. Slice the cake into layers, add the buttercream between the layers
  6. Assemble the layers and frost the sides/top of the cake, put in fridge for
    1 hour before slicing to help cake maintain its shape
